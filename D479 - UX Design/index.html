Travel Tips & FAQs

Currency Taniti uses the U.S. dollar (USD) as its official currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several Banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businessess accept major credit cards.

Language Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English, very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by older residents.

Safety: Violent crime is very rare in Taniti, but as tourism increases, there are more reports of pickpocketing and other petty crimes.

Drinking Laws: The drinking age of Taniti is 18 and the drinking age is not strictly enforced. Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9.00 a.m.
