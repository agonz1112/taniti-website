Restaurants

Taniti has ten restaurants. Five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Grocery Stores

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one conveience store that is open 24 hours a day.
