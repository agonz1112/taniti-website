Frequently Asked Questions

What currency is used

Taniti uses the U.S. Dollar. Many businesses also accept Euros and Japanese Yen.

What voltage are power outlets?

Power outlets are 120 volts, the same as in the United States.

Is Taniti safe?

Violent crime is very rare. Visitors should still be aware of occasional petty theft.

What is the drinking age?

The legal drinking age is 18 years old.

When is alcohol sold?

Alcohol cannot be sold or served between midnight and 9:00 a.m.

Are there medical facilities?

Taniti has one hospital and several clinics. Many hospital employees are multilingual.

Do attractions close on holidays.

Yes. Some attractions and restaurants may close during national holidays.
