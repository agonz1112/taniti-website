Transportation

Learn how to arrive on Taniti and discover the many ways to travel around the island during your stay.

Getting to Taniti

Most visitors arrive by air through Taniti's airport. The airport currently accomodates small jets and propeller aircraft and is being expanded to support larger commercial flights.

Some visitors also arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay once each week.

Getting Around the Island

Public Buses

Public buses serve Taniti City daily from 5:00 a.m. until 11:00 p.m.

Rental Cars

Rental cars are available from an agency located near the airport.

Taxis

Taxis are available throughout Taniti City and popular tourists destinations.

Bike Rentals

Bikes and helmets can be rented from several vendors across the island.

Explore on Foot

Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable. Many tourists also stay near Merriton Landing, where attractions, restaurants, and shops can easily be reached by walking.
